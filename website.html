About Rapid Response Plumbing

Rapid Response Plumbing Services was established in 2010 with a simple mission: to provide reliable, high-quality emergency plumbing services to our community at any hour of the day or night.

What started as a small team of dedicated plumbers has grown into a full-service emergency plumbing company serving the entire metropolitan area. Despite our growth, we maintain the same level of personalized service and attention to detail that has earned us the trust of thousands of satisfied customers.

Our Emergency Plumbing Services

At Rapid Response Plumbing, we handle all types of plumbing emergencies with prompt, professional service. Our fully-equipped team arrives ready to diagnose and fix your problem on the spot.

Burst Pipe Repair

Burst pipes can cause extensive water damage in minutes. Our emergency team will shut off your water supply, repair or replace the damaged pipe, and help minimize water damage to your property.

Common Causes: Freezing temperatures, pipe corrosion, water pressure issues, improper installation

Our Solution: Rapid response with specialized equipment to locate and repair the burst pipe, water extraction services, and preventative recommendations.

Request This Service

Severe Drain Clogs

When plungers and store-bought solutions fail, our professional drain clearing services will get your water flowing again. We use advanced techniques to clear even the most stubborn blockages.

Common Causes: Grease buildup, foreign objects, tree root intrusion, collapsed pipes

Our Solution: Video inspection to locate the clog, hydro jetting or snaking services, preventative maintenance plans.

Request This Service

Water Heater Emergencies

From leaking tanks to complete failures, our technicians can repair or replace your water heater quickly to restore your hot water supply.

Common Issues: Leaking tanks, heating element failures, thermostat problems, pressure valve malfunctions

Our Solution: Fast diagnosis, same-day repairs when possible, emergency replacements with energy-efficient options.

Request This Service

Sewer Line Backups

Sewer backups pose serious health risks and property damage concerns. Our emergency team will clear the blockage and sanitize affected areas.

Warning Signs: Multiple drain clogs, gurgling toilets, sewage odors, water backing up in unusual places

Our Solution: Sewer line camera inspection, powerful clearing equipment, trenchless repair options when needed.

Request This Service

Emergency Leak Detection

Unseen leaks can cause significant structural damage. Our advanced leak detection technology can locate hidden leaks without extensive demolition.

Signs of Hidden Leaks: Unexplained high water bills, water stains, mold growth, warm spots on floors

Our Solution: Non-invasive electronic leak detection, thermal imaging, pressure testing, and precise repairs.
